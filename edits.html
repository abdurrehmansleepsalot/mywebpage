Edits

1.Brightness

It basically adjusts brightness of your picture it is also known as exposure

2.Contrast

It removes haze from pictures and enhances texture also clarity

3.Temprature scale

It adjusts temprature of your picture makes it warm or cool

4.Saturation

It increases or decreases the saturation of colors in a picture

5.Shadows

It increases the exposure of shadows of your pictures it can be very helpful in some situations

6.Highlights

It increases the exposure of white area of your picture it is also known as white in basic adjustments
